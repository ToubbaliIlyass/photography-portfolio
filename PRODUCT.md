# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Potential clients, art directors, gallery curators, and collaborators who discover Ilyass Toubbali's work online. They arrive curious, often from social media or word of mouth. They want to form a quick impression of the photographer's vision and style before deciding to reach out.

## Product Purpose

Abstracta is the personal photography portfolio of Ilyass Toubbali. It exists to make a strong, lasting first impression — to communicate a specific visual voice (street, documentary, abstract) and convert interest into contact. Success means a visitor scrolls through, feels the aesthetic, and sends a message or shares the work.

## Brand Personality

Editorial. Tactile. Quietly confident.

The site should feel like a physical object — a curated zine or a gallery wall — not a web template. Polaroids, film grain, heavy type, amber accents. Nothing generic, nothing noisy.

## Anti-references

- Behance / portfolio grid templates
- Wix photography templates
- Anything with parallax that screams "web agency"
- Busy, colorful UI — this is monochrome with one accent color
- Anything that feels like a SaaS product or a dashboard

## Design Principles

1. **The photo is the product** — UI exists to frame it, never compete with it.
2. **Tactile over digital** — polaroids, paper textures, analog references beat web-native patterns.
3. **One gesture, one moment** — each section should have a single clear interaction or emotion, not a menu of options.
4. **Restraint is strength** — fewer elements, more impact. Every addition needs to earn its place.
5. **Mobile is a different canvas, not a smaller desktop** — the mobile experience should be intentionally redesigned, not shrunken.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Prefers-reduced-motion respected for all decorative animations. Touch targets minimum 44×44px.
